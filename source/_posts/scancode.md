date: 2015-10-16

键位        | 扫描码
------------|-------
ESC         | 00 01
TAB         | 00 0F
CapsLock    | 00 3A
左Shift     | 00 2A
右Shift     | 00 36
左Alt       | 00 38
右Alt       | E0 38
左Ctrl      | 00 1D
右Ctrl      | E0 1D
PrintScreen | E0 37
上矢印      | E0 48
下矢印      | E0 50
右矢印      | E0 4D
左矢印      | E0 4B
Insert      | E0 52
Delete      | E0 53
Home        | E0 47
End         | E0 4F
PageUp      | E0 49
PageDown    | E0 51
左Win key   | E0 5B
右Win key   | E0 5C
application | E0 5D
PAUSE       | 00 45
ScrollLock  | 00 46
